## Tangerine Money-Back Mastercard — Overview
The Tangerine Money-Back Mastercard is a no-annual-fee cash-back card on the Mastercard
network. There is no minimum income requirement. You earn 2% cash back in up to two
spending categories you choose, and 0.5% cash back on all other purchases. A third 2%
category is available if you deposit your reward money into a linked Tangerine Savings
Account. Reward money is paid monthly, applied to your card balance or deposited to savings.

## Tangerine Money-Back Mastercard — Reward Categories
The selectable 2% categories are: groceries, furniture, restaurants, hotel-motel, gas,
recurring bills, drug store, home improvement, entertainment, and public transportation &
parking. You may select two categories at the 2% rate. Depositing reward money into a
Tangerine Savings Account unlocks a third category at 2%. All non-category spend earns
the base rate of 0.5%. There is no cap on the cash back you can earn.

## Tangerine Money-Back Mastercard — Purchase Assurance
New items bought entirely with the Tangerine Money-Back Mastercard are protected against
loss, theft, or damage for the first 90 days from the date of purchase. Coverage is up to
the purchase price of the item, subject to a lifetime maximum of CAD 60,000. Vehicles,
perishables, and items lost without evidence of theft are excluded.

## Tangerine Money-Back Mastercard — Extended Warranty
Extended Warranty doubles the original manufacturer's warranty period, up to a maximum of
one additional year, on items purchased with the card. The manufacturer's warranty must be
five years or less. Coverage is up to the original purchase price. Used items are not eligible.

## Tangerine Money-Back Mastercard — Fraud Protection (Zero Liability)
Through Mastercard Zero Liability, you are not held responsible for unauthorized
transactions made on your account when it is in good standing and you have used reasonable
care to protect your card and PIN. Report unauthorized activity promptly. Some commercial
or anonymous prepaid cards may be excluded.

## Tangerine Money-Back Mastercard — Fees
The annual fee is CAD 0. The standard purchase and cash-advance interest rate is 19.95%.
A foreign transaction fee of 2.5% applies to purchases made in a foreign currency. The
cash-advance fee is CAD 3.50 within Canada and CAD 5.00 outside Canada.

## Tangerine World Mastercard — Overview
The Tangerine World Mastercard is a no-annual-fee premium card on the Mastercard network.
Eligibility requires a minimum personal income of CAD 60,000 or household income of
CAD 100,000. It earns the same 2% category cash back as the Money-Back card and adds
premium travel and insurance benefits, including mobile device insurance, rental car
collision/loss damage waiver, travel medical emergency insurance, and Boingo Wi-Fi access.

## Tangerine World Mastercard — Mobile Device Insurance
Mobile Device Insurance covers loss, theft, or accidental damage to a new mobile device
when the full retail purchase price is charged to the Tangerine World Mastercard. Coverage
is up to CAD 1,000 per claim, with the covered amount depreciating over a two-year period.
Devices costing more than CAD 1,000, cosmetic-only damage, and devices on an unpaid
financing or installment plan are excluded.

## Tangerine World Mastercard — Rental Car Insurance
The Rental Car Collision/Loss Damage Waiver covers damage to or theft of an eligible
rental vehicle when you reserve and pay for the entire rental with the card and decline the
rental agency's collision damage waiver. The rental period must not exceed 31 consecutive
days. Exotic and luxury vehicles, cargo vans, and vehicles older than 12 years are not
covered.

## Tangerine World Mastercard — Travel Medical Insurance
Travel Medical Emergency Insurance provides up to CAD 1,000,000 per insured person for
eligible emergency medical expenses during the first 15 days of a trip outside your home
province. Coverage applies to cardholders under the age of 65. Pre-existing conditions,
trips longer than 15 days, and cardholders aged 65 or older are not covered.

## Tangerine World Mastercard — Purchase Assurance and Extended Warranty
The Tangerine World Mastercard includes the same Purchase Assurance (90-day loss, theft, or
damage protection up to a CAD 60,000 lifetime maximum) and Extended Warranty (doubles the
manufacturer's warranty up to one additional year) as the Money-Back card.

## Tangerine World Mastercard — Boingo Wi-Fi
Boingo Wi-Fi for Mastercard gives World Mastercard holders unlimited access to a global
network of more than one million Wi-Fi hotspots across up to four devices. You must enroll
your eligible card once at the Boingo portal to activate the benefit.

## Tangerine World Mastercard — Fees
The annual fee is CAD 0. The standard purchase and cash-advance interest rate is 19.95%.
A foreign transaction fee of 2.5% applies to purchases made in a foreign currency. The
cash-advance fee is CAD 3.50 within Canada and CAD 5.00 outside Canada.
